About Me

I am an Scrum Master with a 8 years+ in the software industry. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with clients globally and collaborate with multiple Product teams.

In my approach to teamwork, I firmly believe in recognizing and leveraging the unique strengths that each individual brings. By placing team members in roles that align with their strengths and serving as a servant leader, I enable them to shine and contribute in their own unique ways, much like the vibrant colors of a rainbow. This collaborative spirit not only benefits the team but also drives greater achievements and success for the organization.

I prioritize kindness and courtesy in all interactions, fostering a positive and respectful environment. Additionally, I emphasize the importance of diligence and striving for excellence as we work towards our goals.

Together, let's create a harmonious and high-performing team where everyone can thrive and achieve outstanding results.
